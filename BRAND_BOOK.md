# Sourcery Brand Book & Design System

## Core Identity
**Design Philosophy**: "The New Standard" - Clean, minimalist, high-contrast, premium SaaS aesthetic. Inspired by top-tier Framer sites.

## Typography
- **Headlines (H1, H2, H3)**: `Almarai`, Bold.
  - *Hierarchy*:
    - **H1 (Hero)**: 64px, Leading 1.1, Tracking -0.02em (Titlecase).
    - **H2 (Section)**: 40px, Leading 1.1, Tracking -0.01em.
    - **H3 (Card Title)**: 24px, Leading 1.2.
- **Body Text**: `Almarai`, Regular.
  - *Size*: 16px (or 50% of headline scale for specific layouts).
  - *Leading*: 1.6 (High legibility).
- **Accents/Subheaders**: `Geist Mono`, Bold.
  - *Usage*: Badges, labels, small upper-case details.

## Color Palette
- **Deep Dark (Primary Bg)**: `#05191C` (Rich, deep teal-black).
- **Brand Cyan**: `#00CADC` (Vibrant, used for primary actions and accents).
- **Paper (Light Bg)**: `#F0EFEB` (Off-white for warmth).
- **Ink (Light Accent)**: `#D1F9FF` (Very light cyan for subtle overlays).

## Spacing & Layout
- **Max Container Width**: 1200px.
- **Section Vertical Padding**: 120px (Desktop), 80px (Mobile).
- **Grid Gaps**: 32px (Standard card spacing).
- **Border Radius**: 
  - Cards: 32px.
  - Buttons: 999px (Full rounded).

## Component Rules
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: `#00CADC` background, `#05191C` text, Bold.
  - Ghost: Border `#00CADC` or subtle translucent, Cyan text.
- **Cards**:
  - Subtle border: 1px solid `rgba(0, 202, 220, 0.1)`.
  - Shadow: Soft, large-spread shadows (e.g., `0 20px 40px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)`).
- **Animations**:
  - **Scroll Reveal**: Fade-in and translate-up (20px) with cubic-bezier easing.
  - **Hover**: Subtle scale (1.02) and shadow deepening.
